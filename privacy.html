Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

1. Introduction

Burmese Dub Bot ("the Service") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

When you use the Service, we may collect the following information:

  • Telegram User Data: Your Telegram user ID, first name, and chat ID for the purpose of receiving and responding to your messages.
  • Video URLs: Links to videos you submit for processing (YouTube, TikTok, Xiaohongshu).
  • Processed Content: Transcripts, translated text, voiceover audio, and final dubbed videos generated during processing.
  • TikTok Account Data: When you authorize TikTok integration, we access your TikTok account to upload videos on your behalf, as permitted by the TikTok Content Posting API.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To process your video dubbing requests and deliver the translated videos.
  • To upload dubbed videos to your connected social media accounts (e.g., TikTok) at your request.
  • To store processed videos temporarily on our servers and Google Drive for delivery.
  • To communicate with you via Telegram regarding the status of your requests.

4. Data Storage and Retention

  • Processed video files are stored on our servers and Google Drive for delivery purposes.
  • We do not permanently store your Telegram messages or personal conversations.
  • Video processing data (audio, transcripts, temporary files) may be retained on our servers.
  • You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Third-Party Services: We use Google Drive (for file storage), Telegram (for communication), Google Gemini (for translation), and TikTok API (for video publishing). These services have their own privacy policies.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

6. TikTok Data Usage

When you connect your TikTok account to the Service:

  • We only access the permissions you explicitly grant (video upload and publish).
  • We do not access your TikTok messages, followers, or other personal data.
  • We only upload videos that you explicitly request to be published.
  • You can revoke access at any time through TikTok's settings.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including secure server connections (SSH), encrypted API communications (HTTPS), and access controls. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

8. Your Rights

  • You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data.
  • You may revoke TikTok authorization at any time.
  • You may stop using the Service at any time by ceasing to interact with the Telegram bot.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or to exercise your data rights, please contact us via the Telegram bot or at the email provided in the Service.
